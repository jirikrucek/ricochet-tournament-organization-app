# Bracket Structure

Fixed 32-player Brazilian double elimination with full placement brackets.

## Main Brackets

- **Winners Bracket (WB):** 5 rounds
  - R1: 16 matches
  - R2: 8 matches  
  - R3: 4 matches
  - R4: 2 matches
  - R5: 1 match (WB Final)

- **Losers Bracket (LB):** 6 rounds + LB Final
  - R1-R6: Progressively fewer matches
  - lb-final: Gateway to consolation final

## Placement Brackets

Every player gets a final placement:
- `p25` — Places 25-28
- `p29` — Places 29-30  
- `p31` — Places 31-32
- `p17` — Places 17-20
- `p21` — Places 21-24
- `p13` — Places 13-16
- `p9` — Places 9-12
- `p7` — 7th-8th place
- `p5` — 5th-6th place  
- `p4` — 4th place

## Finals

- `consolation-final` — 3rd place qualifier (WB R5 loser vs LB Final winner)
- `grand-final` — Championship (WB R5 winner vs Consolation Final winner)

**Total:** 127 matches for complete 1-32 ranking
