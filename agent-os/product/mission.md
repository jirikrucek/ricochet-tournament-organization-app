# Product Mission

## Problem

Managing complex double-elimination tournaments manually is time-consuming and error-prone. Tournament organizers need efficient tools to handle player registration, bracket management, match scoring, and real-time updates for spectators.

## Target Users

This product serves three primary audiences:

- **Tournament Organizers**: Need administrative tools to manage tournaments, players, matches, and brackets
- **Spectators**: Want to follow live tournament progress and view match results
- **Competitive Ricochet Players**: Require access to their profiles, statistics, and tournament standings

## Solution

A comprehensive web-based tournament management system that combines:

- **Dual-mode architecture**: Works offline with localStorage or online with Supabase, ensuring reliable operation regardless of internet connectivity
- **Specialized Brazilian double-elimination bracket system**: Purpose-built for Ricochet tournament format with precise drop patterns and match progression
- **Multi-language support**: Accessible to international audiences with 5 language options
- **Real-time updates**: Live match tracking and instant bracket visualization
